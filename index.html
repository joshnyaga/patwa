Terms of Service

  • The company shall not be liable for items that were stolen but have been found and brought to Patwa.
  • The Company shall not be held responsible for any item that was lost and brought in damaged.
  • Anyone who brings in a lost item will be appreciated after the owner has reclaimed the item and has verified it was not stolen by the one who brought it in.
  • Details of lost items will be kept confidential to the company until the rightful owner has been identified.
  • Any item brought to the Company will be kept until the owner reclaims it..
  • Items will be handed over to the owner once he/she has paid the service fee as per the nature of the item in the company’s price list.
  • A Lost Item will stay in the Patwa office where for a period of 3 months. After which, if not reclaimed will accrue interest as per the stay.
